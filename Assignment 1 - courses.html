Computer Programming Curriculum

  • Level 01 - CST8116 - Introduction to Computer Programming

    Introduction to the fundamentals of logic, problem solving, and programming language structure.

  • Level 01 - CST8118 - Computer Essentials

    Survey of computer maintenance, operating systems and software, security, and environmental impacts of technology.

  • Level 01 - CST8215 - Introduction to Database

    Fundamentals of relational database using Entity Relation Diagrams and Structured Query Language to create, modify, and query databases.

  • Level 01 - CST8300 - Achieving Success in Changing Environments

    Exploration of future possibilities, self-assessment of aptitudes and strengths, and application of critical thinking to address societal issues.

  • Level 01 - ENL1813T - Communications I

    Practice of writing, speaking, reading, listening, locating and documenting information to strengthen professional communication.

  • Level 01 - MAT8001C - Technical Mathematics for Computer Science

    Study of algebraic and trancendental functions, computer number systems, Boolean algebra and vector components.

  • Level 02 - CST2355 - Database Systems

    Experience object-relational database management systems (Oracle and MySQL) using concepts such as case structure, rollup and cube operations.

  • Level 02 - CST8102 - Operating System Fundamentals (GNU/Linux)

    Examination of operating systems structure, process management, storage, installation, configuration, and administration based on GNU/Linux.

  • Level 02 - CST8284 - Object Oriented Programming (Java)

    Understanding of object-oriented concepts such as encapsulation, inheritance, abstraction, and polymorphism with practical applications.

  • Level 02 - CST8285 - Web Programming

    Development of basic skills of web programming, design, and implementation through JavaScript, HTML5, PHP, and CSS.

  • Level 02 - ENL2019T - Technical Communication for Engineering Technologies

    Fostering independent and collaborative thinking, research, writing, visual communication, and presentation skills.

  • Level 02 - GEP1001 - Cooperative Education and Job Readiness

    Preparation for conducting a professional job search and success in the workplace through workshop-style sessions.
